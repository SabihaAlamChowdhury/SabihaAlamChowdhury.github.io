Research Interests

Exploring innovative approaches at the intersection of AI, reasoning, and multimodal learning

My research focuses on Neurosymbolic AI, which combines the pattern recognition capabilities of neural networks with the explicit reasoning power of symbolic systems. I am particularly interested in Multimodal Deep Learning that integrates Computer Vision (CV) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to enable machines to understand and interact with the world more naturally.

Additionally, I investigate Cybersecurity in Digital Twins, exploring how virtual replicas of physical systems can be secured against emerging threats, and Intelligent Automation systems that leverage AI to optimize complex processes in urban logistics, healthcare, and workplace safety.
